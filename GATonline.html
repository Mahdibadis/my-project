what is GTA Online

GTA Online isn’t just a game — it’s a massive online world where you live out your criminal fantasy. Think of it like a crime simulator mixed with freedom, chaos, and strategy. You start from the bottom, with no money, no weapons, and no respect. Your goal? Climb to the top — become rich, feared, stylish, and powerful. You do that by: 💰 Building your empire (CEO, nightclub owner, drug kingpin, arms dealer) 🚗 Owning luxury cars, jets, yachts, and mansions 💣 Dominating other players in battles and missions 🤝 Controlling the streets with your crew or alone It’s like becoming a legend in Los Santos — your story, your way.

Step 1: Early Game Money (Level 1–30)

While you're still new, focus on fast money and low risk:
  • Contact Missions (from Lamar, Gerald, Simeon)
  • Treasure Hunts (get a free Red Dead gun worth big RP & cash)
  • VIP Work (once you have $50,000 — register as VIP from the Interaction Menu)
  • Time Trials ($100,000 in less than 2 mins if you drive well)
  • Double/Triple Money Events (check weekly bonuses on the Rockstar site or in-game)
