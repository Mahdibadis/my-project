GTA 5 - Introduction

Grand Theft Auto V, also known as GTA 5, is one of the most iconic and successful video games ever made. Developed by Rockstar Games and released in 2013, it redefined what an open-world game could be. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, the game brings players into the sprawling, vibrant city of Los Santos — a world inspired by Los Angeles, full of crime, chaos, and endless possibilities. What makes GTA 5 truly unique is its three playable protagonists:

  • Michael De Santa – A retired bank robber trying to live a quiet life with his dysfunctional family.
  • Franklin Clinton – A street-smart hustler looking to rise above the gang life.
  • Trevor Philips – A wild and unpredictable criminal who thrives in chaos and violence.

These three lives collide in a series of explosive missions, daring heists, betrayals, and intense drama. The player can switch between characters anytime, exploring the story from multiple angles. Aside from the gripping story mode, GTA 5 also features GTA Online, a massive multiplayer world where players can create their own character, build an empire, race cars, rob banks, and team up with friends in epic missions. ith its breathtaking graphics, detailed world, and freedom of choice, GTA 5 isn't just a game — it's an experience.
