Rules.

1 No RDM (Random Death Match): ❌ You can't kill someone for no reason. ✅ You must have a strong RP reason.

2 No VDM (Vehicle Death Match) ❌ Don’t hit people with your car just for fun. ✅ You can only do it if it makes sense in RP (like running from cops and you crash accidentally).

3. No Fail RP ❌ Doing unrealistic things your character wouldn’t do. ✅ Example of FailRP: Pulling a gun out of nowhere while handcuffed.

4. No Power Gaming ❌ Forcing actions that give you unfair advantages. ✅ Example: “/me knocks you out instantly” — Not allowed.

5. No Meta Gaming ❌ Using real-life or outside info in-game. ✅ Example: Watching someone’s stream to find them or using Discord voice to give info is illegal.

6. No Combat Logging ❌ Leaving the game to escape RP or avoid arrest, death, etc. ✅ Always finish the scene even if you're losing.

7. No Breaking Character (OOC) ❌ You must always act as your character in the game. ✅ Only use /ooc (out-of-character chat) when needed.

8. Fear RP ✅ If someone points a gun at you, act scared and obey. ❌ Don’t act like a superhero when you're outnumbered or helpless.
